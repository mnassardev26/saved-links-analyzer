## Description

Saved Links Analyzer helps you turn your Facebook Saved list into an organized, searchable library.

Open Facebook Saved, launch the extension side panel, and scan the items visible on the page. The extension collects saved-link metadata locally in Chrome so you can review, filter, favorite, categorize, and export your saved items without sending them to an external service.

Use it when your saved list has become hard to browse, when you want to find older saved posts faster, or when you need a CSV export of links you have collected over time.

Key features:

- Scan visible Facebook Saved items from the Chrome side panel.
- Auto-scroll to collect larger saved-link lists.
- Search and filter by title, source, category, item type, favorite status, thumbnails, duration, and collection date.
- Automatically group items into practical categories such as Learning, Work/Career, Business/Money, Food, Health/Fitness, Entertainment, Travel, Shopping, Social/Personal, and Other.
- Favorite important items for faster follow-up.
- Export filtered or selected saved items to CSV.
- Remove saved items from Facebook when the matching item is visible on the current saved page.
- Clear scanned data from the extension settings panel whenever needed.

Privacy:

Saved Links Analyzer is local-first. Scanned metadata is stored in Chrome extension storage on your device. The extension does not use analytics, does not send saved-link data to a backend, and does not share your saved list with third-party AI services.

Permissions:

- Storage is used to keep scanned saved-link metadata locally in Chrome.
- Side panel is used to display the organizer next to Facebook.
- Facebook host access is used only for Facebook Saved pages so the extension can scan visible saved items and interact with visible remove controls when requested.
